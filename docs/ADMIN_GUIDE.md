## What You Can Edit

### 1. **Customer Testimonials**

**Path:** Testimonials ‚Üí New Testimonial

**What to add:**

- Customer name (e.g., "Sarah Johnson")
- Star rating (1-5 stars)
- Review text (what they said)
- Service type (what you did for them)
- Date (when the job was done)
- Photo (optional - before/after pic)

**Example:**

```
Name: John Smith
Rating: 5
Text: "Dee and her team were amazing! Cleaned out my entire garage in 2 hours. Professional, affordable, and eco-friendly. Highly recommend!"
Service: Garage Cleanout
Date: 01/15/2026
```

## Step-by-Step: Adding Your First Testimonial

1. **Log in** to admin panel
2. Click **"Testimonials"** in left menu
3. Click **"New Testimonial"** button
4. Fill in fields:
   - **Customer Name:** "Jennifer Williams"
   - **Rating:** 5
   - **Review Text:** "Quick, professional, great price!"
   - **Service Type:** Select "Junk Removal"
   - **Date:** Pick the date
5. Click **"Publish"** button (top right)
6. Done! It appears on your website immediately

---

## Step-by-Step: Updating Your Phone Number

1. **Log in** to admin panel
2. Click **"Business Settings"** in left menu
3. Click **"Contact Information"**
4. Find **"Business Phone"** field
5. Change from `4075558585` to your real number
6. Change **"Phone Display"** to how you want it shown, like `(407) 555-JUNK`
7. Click **"Publish"**
8. Phone number updates across entire website!

## Common Tasks

### Adding 5 Customer Reviews

**Time:** 10 minutes

1. Log in to admin
2. Go to Testimonials
3. For each review:
   - Click "New Testimonial"
   - Copy/paste customer name
   - Copy/paste review text
   - Select star rating
   - Click Publish
4. Repeat 5 times

**Pro tip:** Keep customer reviews in a text file, copy/paste into admin panel all at once.

## Troubleshooting

### "I forgot my password"

1. Go to /admin/
2. Click "Forgot password?"
3. Enter your email
4. Check email for reset link
5. Set new password

### "Changes aren't showing on website"

1. Make sure you clicked "Publish" (not just "Save")
2. Wait 2-3 minutes for rebuild
3. Hard refresh browser (Ctrl+Shift+R on Windows, Cmd+Shift+R on Mac)
4. Still not working? Text Jake

### "I accidentally deleted something"

1. Don't panic!
2. Contact Jake
3. We can restore from backups
4. Nothing is permanently lost
