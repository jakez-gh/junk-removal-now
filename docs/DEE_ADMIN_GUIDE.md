## ğŸ”„ Editorial Workflow (How Changes Are Published)

Your site uses **enterprise-grade security** with an editorial workflow:

1. **Draft**: You make changes
2. **In Review**: Changes are saved but not live yet
3. **Ready**: You approve the changes
4. **Published**: Changes go live on your website

### To Publish Your Changes:

1. Save your edits
2. Click "Set status to Ready" at the top
3. Click "Publish" to make changes live

> This prevents accidental changes from immediately appearing on your live site.
